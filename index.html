Nano-optical conveyor belt

Professor Hesselink's group focuses its research on fundamental processes related to laser-matter interaction with novel applications in telecom technology. The Hesselink group is investigating fundamental materials processes in electro-optic media for manipulation of the refractive index and modification of the interaction between waves and grating structures for WDM switching applications. They have also pioneered the development of digital holographic data storage systems.
